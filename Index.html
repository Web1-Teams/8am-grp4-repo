Different Nutrition Systems

Nutrition System Focus Key Benefits Typical Foods
Balanced Diet All-around health Promotes overall well-being, supports immune function, energy balance Vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats
Low-Carb Diet Weight loss, blood sugar control Helps with weight loss, reduces blood sugar spikes Meat, fish, eggs, leafy greens, nuts, seeds
Ketogenic Diet Fat-burning, energy from fats Supports fat burning, improves brain function, reduces seizures Fatty fish, butter, avocado, nuts, oils, low-carb veggies
Plant-Based Diet Environmental health, ethical eating Reduces risk of chronic diseases, supports heart health Vegetables, fruits, legumes, nuts, seeds, whole grains
Paleo Diet Eating like our ancestors Improves digestion, promotes weight loss Meat, fish, eggs, vegetables, fruits, nuts
Intermittent Fasting Weight loss, metabolic health Helps with weight loss, improves insulin sensitivity Depends on eating window, typically normal meals
